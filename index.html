About SHIELD

Intelligent tools for a better life with diabetes

SHIELD is a bioengineering research group at the Department of Information Engineering, University of Padova, led by Prof. Andrea Facchinetti. We develop AI-powered decision support systems for Type 1 Diabetes (T1D) — predictive algorithms, intelligent bolus calculators, and digital twin frameworks that learn from each patient's physiological and behavioural data to anticipate glucose excursions and optimise therapy in real time.

Across all our tools, eXplainable AI (XAI) and clinical usability are first-class requirements. Every recommendation must be transparent and trustworthy for both patients and clinicians. Our work spans the full pipeline: from raw CGM and wearable signals to validated, deployable digital health solutions.
